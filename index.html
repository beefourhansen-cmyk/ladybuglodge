A Place Unlike Any Other

Perched on the narrows connecting Churchill and Peter Pond Lakes in Buffalo Narrows, Saskatchewan, Ladybug Lodge is a fully-equipped, four-season retreat that proves the wilderness and comfort aren't mutually exclusive. Northern Lights overhead, world-class fishing out front, and every creature comfort waiting inside. Come stay a while.

The Experience

Fully Furnished Creature Comforts

Two bedroom (one King, one Queen), and full bathroom complete with clean, fresh linens. Washer and dryer along with fully equipped kitchen complete with stove, microwave, dishwasher, all utensils, and plates. A big screen TV, surround sound, reclining leather seats, and even a robotic vacuum await you for the stay of a lifetime! Even the coffee connoisseur will have access to an espresso machine. Just because you're in the wilderness doesn't mean you can't have environmentally sustainable luxury!

Local Geological Phenomenon

Buffalo Narrows is home to over 100 miles (160km) of fine, white sand beach — a geological oddity from sand dunes that date back millions of years.

1,000 Sq. Ft (92.9 m²)

Large enough to comfortably sleep seven or more. The more the merrier! Two bedrooms, one bathroom, fully furnished.

Northern Lights Viewing

Northern Lights viewing anyone? If the Aurora Borealis is calling your name, all you need to do is look skyward at Ladybug Lodge any given night to experience the magic of the Northern Lights (weather permitting). This four-season getaway will ensure you experience all the magic the sky has to offer.

World Class Fishing

Ladybug Lodge is located on the narrows, which connects Churchill and Peter Pond Lakes; both gateways to world class fishing. Winter trip? Ice fishing is an option, and the narrows never freeze, so you can cast your reel into open water year-round!

Stay Connected

Ladybug Lodge is equipped with Starlink Internet through SpaceX allowing for access to Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney Plus, and offers plenty of high-speed bandwidth for those who need to stay connected while they work from home.
