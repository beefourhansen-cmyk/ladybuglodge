A Place to Breathe

Tucked along the water's edge, Ladybug Lodge is a lovingly maintained cottage with a story behind every board. Named for the man who built it, it's a place where families slow down, kids run barefoot, and the only agenda is the sunset. Come stay a while.

The Experience

🌊

Private Waterfront

Step off the deck and straight into the lake. Your own quiet stretch of shoreline — no crowds, no noise.

🛶

Paddle & Explore

Canoe and kayak included with your stay. The lake is calm, the mornings are misty, and the herons are everywhere.

🔥

Firepit Nights

Gather around the firepit as the stars come out. Roast marshmallows, tell stories, and lose track of time.

🛏️

Comfortable Beds

Sleeps up to 8 guests across multiple bedrooms. Cozy, clean, and ready for your whole crew.

🍳

Full Kitchen

Everything you need to cook a proper meal. Bring groceries, make memories, eat well.

📵

Truly Unplugged

No distractions. Just the lake, the trees, and the people you came with. This is what vacation is supposed to feel like.
